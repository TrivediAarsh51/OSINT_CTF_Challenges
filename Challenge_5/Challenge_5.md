# Briefing

**Greetings, Special Agent.**

A major security incident has unfolded.

Earlier today, at approximately **09:15 local time**, an armed extremist group launched a coordinated assault on a five-star hotel hosting an international business summit. The attackers breached the premises during peak occupancy and quickly took control of the building, holding hundreds of guests and staff inside.

Despite a rapid response from security forces, the operation ended in severe casualties. **More than 150 individuals were confirmed dead**, with many others injured during the incident. Several assailants were neutralized on-site, but intelligence confirms that the **primary orchestrator of the attack remains at large**.

During the post-operation sweep, investigators recovered a small amount of digital evidence from the scene. Among it is a **photograph of the hotel taken shortly after the attack**, believed to have been captured in close proximity to the perpetrators during the chaos. Analysts suspect that the image may contain overlooked details that could assist in identifying the location tied to the individual responsible for planning the operation.

In parallel, intelligence agencies have also released a **separate image of a suspected operative believed to be connected to the incident**. The individual’s identity remains unconfirmed, but field reports suggest strong ties to the region where the attack was coordinated.

Your task is to assist in the ongoing investigation by analyzing all available evidence. Correlate findings from the recovered materials, establish the common link between them, and determine the location associated with the operation. This may ultimately lead to the identification of the mastermind behind the attack.

The **flag** for this challenge is the **Primary Suspect's Birth Place**.

Every detail matters, Special Agent. In investigations like these, truth is rarely given — it must be uncovered.
